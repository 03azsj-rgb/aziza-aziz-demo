About Mukhaweer Yadooh

A new name, carrying an old feeling.

Mukhaweer Yadooh was established in the UAE in 2026 with one simple purpose: to create clothing that feels connected to home. Our collections bring together graceful abayas, colorful Emirati Mukhawaras, and the comfort of beautifully made pajamas.

We design for women who value tradition without standing still — pieces that feel local, personal, and easy to make your own.
